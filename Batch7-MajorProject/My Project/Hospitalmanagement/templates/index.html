Welcome to Rammohan Childrens Hospital

Our health care institution provides patient treatment with specialized health science , auxiliary healthcare staff and medical equipment.

Our hospital typically is the major health care facility in its region, with many beds for intensive care and additional beds for patients who need long-term care.

We like to think that we are an extraordinary practice that is all about you - your potential, your comfort, your health, and your individuality. You are important to us and we strive to help you in every and any way that we can.

Our Doctor

Our hospital and doctor are very well trained when it comes to managing the physical, behavioural and mental care for children from birth until age 18.



Our doctor, Dr.Rammohan specializes in diagnosing and treating a broad range of childhood illness, from minor health problems to serious diseases.
